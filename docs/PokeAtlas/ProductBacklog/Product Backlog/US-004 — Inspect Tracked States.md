# US-004 — Inspect Tracked States

Epic: Collection Tracking
Priority: P1
Status: Not started

**As a** trainer, **I want to** inspect tracked states for a Pokémon. **So that** I can understand what collectible targets I already completed.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Trainer can long press a Pokémon card in mobile or hover on desktop.
- System can display tracked states.

## Examples

> Bulbasaur

Tracked:

- Shiny
- Hundo
- Shiny Hundo

Long press is informational only.
